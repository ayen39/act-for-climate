About Climate Action

Climate action focuses on reducing environmental damage and promoting sustainability. It involves communities, governments, and individuals working together to protect the Earth.

Problem Statement

South Sudan is increasingly affected by climate change, including flooding, drought, deforestation, and unpredictable weather patterns. These changes damage agriculture, reduce food production, and threaten livelihoods, especially in rural communities.
