The Problem

A Changing Climate in South Sudan

South Sudan is increasingly affected by climate change, including severe flooding, prolonged drought, rapid deforestation, and highly unpredictable weather patterns.

These changes critically damage agriculture, drastically reduce food production, and threaten the core livelihoods of our people, especially within vulnerable rural communities.

Why it matters now

  • ✖
    Livelihoods are being washed away by extreme floods.
  • ✖
    Food security is dropping due to agricultural damage.

How We Fix It
